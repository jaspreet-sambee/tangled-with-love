Our story

The Heart Behind the Stitches

For our mother, every loop of yarn is a story of resilience and love. Her journey began decades ago in the vibrant streets of Amritsar, Punjab. As the eldest sibling, she stepped up with a quiet strength, using her hands to help provide for her family. What started as a necessary skill soon blossomed into a lifelong passion that has traveled with her across oceans.

Now, in the quiet, snowy winters of Canada, those same hands haven't slowed down. When the world outside turns white and cold, she finds her warmth in the rhythmic click of needles and the soft glide of a crochet hook. While she once created out of a sense of responsibility to her parents and siblings, and later as a devoted wife and mother, she now creates for the purest reason of all: herself.

Though technology can be a maze, she has found her own way, turning to her favorite corner of the internet to discover new patterns and techniques. Each day is an exploration of color and texture as she taps into a creative spirit that was always there, waiting for its moment to shine.

This website is a gallery of that journey. It is a celebration of a woman who gave everything to her family and is now weaving her own dreams, one stitch at a time. We hope you feel the warmth and history in every piece she creates.

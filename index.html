Our story

The heart behind the stitches

For our mother, every loop of yarn is a story of resilience and love. Her journey began decades ago in the vibrant streets of Amritsar, Punjab. As the eldest sibling, she stepped up with a quiet strength, using her hands to help provide for her family. What started as a necessary skill soon blossomed into a lifelong passion that has traveled with her across oceans.

Now, in the quiet, snowy winters of Canada, those same hands haven't slowed down. While she once created out of a sense of responsibility to her parents and siblings, and later as a devoted wife and mother, she now creates for the purest reason of all: herself.

This website is a gallery of that journey — a celebration of a woman who gave everything to her family and is now weaving her own dreams, one stitch at a time.
